Our History

Lindemann is a german/swedish industrial metal band created in 2013. The origin fo the name of the band was a random on. It was the first name that came up among the members.Even though the band was formed in 2013, the two main members already knew each other since 2000 and have become great friends throughout the years.

The Members

Till Lindemann is the lead singer of the band and also one of the founding members of the famous neue deutsche härte band Rammstein(since 1994). Lindemann is also a poet, composer and actor. Alf Peter Tägtgren is a swedish musician and producer. He is also the founder, main composer, singer and guitarrist of the death metal band Hypocrisy(since 1990).
